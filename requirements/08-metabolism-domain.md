# Metabolism Domain

## Purpose

Explain how food, exercise, sleep, and timing interact using simplified, educational models. No medical or diagnostic claims.

## Inputs

- Meals and timing
- Exercises and activity
- Sleep quality and duration
- Body weight trends
- Declared goals

## Modeled States (Estimated)

- Energy availability
- Glycogen status
- Insulin activity
- Recovery state
- Fatigue level

## Outputs

- Current metabolic context
- Daily summaries
- Educational explanations
- Goal-adjusted interpretations
